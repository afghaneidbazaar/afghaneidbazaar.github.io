When and where does the bazaar take place?

expand_more

The Afghan Eid Bazaar & Concert 2026 takes place at Royal Palace Banquet Hall — 7845 Edmonds St, Burnaby, BC V3N 1B9 on May 25 & 26, 2026.

Doors open daily from 11:00 AM until midnight. Day 2 features a live Traditional Afghan Music Concert from 8:00 PM to midnight with Hamid Jalali.

Is there an entry fee?

expand_more

Day 1 (May 25) is free admission for everyone. Day 2 (May 26) concert night is a ticketed experience featuring live Afghan music by Hamid Jalali from 8:00 PM to midnight.

How can I apply to be a vendor?

expand_more

We welcome vendors offering traditional Afghan clothing, jewelry, handcrafts, food and Afghan products. Vendor tables are $200 per table per day. Contact us at farooazizi@icloud.com or +1 (672) 673-2533 to reserve your spot.
