When and where does the bazaar take place?

expand_more

The Afghan Eid Bazaar is held annually at the Heritage Pavilion Grounds. This year's celebration runs from June 14th to June 16th.

Doors open daily from 10:00 AM until 9:00 PM. We recommend arriving early to experience the morning Sufi music sets.

Is there an entry fee?

expand_more

General admission is free for all attendees to ensure the cultural heritage remains accessible. However, some specialized workshop sessions in the Cultural Gallery and premium seating for the Evening Attan performances require a separate ticket purchase.

How can I apply to be a vendor?

expand_more

We welcome artisans specializing in traditional Afghan crafts, textiles, and cuisine. Applications are processed through our curated portal.

What traditional food will be available?

expand_more

Authentic Mains

Kabuli Pulao, Mantu (dumplings), and various artisanal kebabs.

Sweet Delicacies

Sheer Khurma, Firni with rosewater, and traditional saffron tea.
